Dana Kirchmar

Women in Aviation and Aerospace Keynote Speaker

"I didn't study the aviation leadership gap from the outside. I lived inside it for thirty-seven years."

About Dana

Dana Kirchmar is a 37-year aviation and aerospace veteran with technical and executive experience at Lockheed Martin, Honeywell, and Mission Control. She has navigated every level of a male-dominated industry and built a career defined by operational excellence. Her talks translate that experience into frameworks that help women in aviation advance — and give aviation leaders the operational case for solving their most expensive retention problem. She has spoken at Women in Aviation International, Aircraft Interiors Expo, and the Rocky Mountain AIAA Conference.
